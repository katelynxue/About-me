I have been with this team since I was in 8th grade, and I have had a blast throughout all 5 years here. In 2023 we won the ISL Championship, which was the first time in over a decade.

I am grateful for the coaches I have had and the experiences I've gained for playing for so many years.

Being the youngest on the team, I was often very shy and wouldn't talk to anyone. Now being the oldest, I often take on the leadership role and help direct the younger teammates.

Milton volleyball has taught me lots of skills about leadership and I will always come back and visit whenever I can.

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen is a Belgian-born Dutch racing driver who competes in F1 for Red Bull Racing. Back to Hobbies

Career summary: Max began karting at age four and quickly progresses through junior categories, winning multiple championships. He made his F1 debut in 2015 with Toro Rosso at the age of 17, becoming the youngest driver in F1 history to compete in a race. In 2016, Max was promoted to Red Bull Racing and famously won on his debut with the team at the Spanish Grand Prix, becoming the youngest race winner in F1 history at 18 years and 228 days. He won his first F1 Drivers World Championship in 2021 after a fierce season-long abttle with Lewis Hamilton, culminating in a controversial victory at the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix. Max has since secured 3 more consecutive titles iin 2022, 2023, and 2024, cementing his status as one fo the most successful drivers in the sports history. As of the 2025 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Max has achieved 67 race wins, 46 pole positions, 35 fastest laps, and 120 podiums in F1. He is known for his aggressive driving style, precise control, and exceptional ability in wet weather conditions.

Personal life: Verstappen was born to former F1 driver Jos Verstappen and competitve kart driver Sophie Kumpen. He has a close relationship with his family, including his younger sister Victoria and 2 younger half-siblings. He is currently married to Kelly Piquet, and welcomed their first child, a daughter named Lily in May 2025. Max has also developed a close bond with Kelly's daughter from a previous relationship, Penelope.

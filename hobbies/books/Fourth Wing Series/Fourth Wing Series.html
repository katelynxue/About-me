Fourth Wing

Fourth Wing is fantasy series written by Rebecca Yarros Back to Books

The story sends you into the brutal and elite world of Basgiath War College, where cadets train to become dragon riders-but a majority don't survive the training. We follow Violet Sorrengail, who, despite being smaller and more frigile, is forced to comete by her mother. In a cutthroat enviroment, Violet must rely on her wits to survive.

Brutal, addictive atmosphere: The setting is relentless, with challenges designed to weed out the weak. Every day is life-or-death gamble, creating intense, fast-paced read that will have you on the edge of your seat.

A powerful, authentic heroine: Violet isn't a typical heroine. Her physical vulnerabilities maker her an underdog is almost every situation, but her intelligence and resilience make her a compelling protagonists. Her journey resonates with readers deeply.

Enemies-to-lovers romance: The tension between Violet and the powerful wingleader Xaden Riorson is electric. Their relationship is built on must animosity and family history, and watching it evolve adds another layer of emtional depth.

Dragons with personaility: The dragons in this story are not just transportation-they are formidable characters in their own right, with personailites and demands. The proccess of bonding with a dragon adds a layer of lore and wonder to the plot.

Verdict: Fourth Wing is a must read for any fantasy/romance readers. With its unforgettable characters, fierce romance, and world-building, its the book you'll stay up reading all night.

Rating: 9/10

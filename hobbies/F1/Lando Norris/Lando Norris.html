Lando Norris

Lando Norris is a British racing driver currently competing in F1 for McLaren. He was born in Bristol, England, andis widely regarded as one of the most exciting young talents in F1. Back to F1

Career summary: Lando began karting at age eight and achieved success, inclduing winning the CIK-FIA KF World Championship in 2014. He advanced through junior formulas, winning championships in MSA Formula and FIA Formula 3, and was runner-up in FIA Formula 2. He made his F1 debut with McLaren in 2019. His achievements include his first F1 podium at the 2020 Austrian Grand Prix and his first pole position at the 2021 Russian Grand Prix. In 2024, he won his first F1 Grand Prix in Miami and finished seond in teh Drivers' World Championship. As of the 2025 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Norris has 9 wins, 13 poles, and 39 podiums in F1. He is recongnized for his smooth yet aggressive driving style and consistent performance.

Personal life: Norris holds both British and Belgian citizenship. he lives in Monaco. He is known for his humor and engageing personality.
